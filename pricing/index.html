Frequently asked questions

What should I bring with me my first day?

Your laptop, a smile, and a willingness to become a CO+HOOTIAN. We provide free hot coffee, wifi, and a kitchen/break room should you choose to bring lunch with you.

Can I pick my desk?

Nest members can choose their own dedicated desk space, and Flex members are welcome to work at any of our flex spaces or non-dedicated desks.

Do I need a key to get into the building?

Depends, do you know the secret password? Just kidding! Key access is granted to Nest and Office members through our fancy-schmancy app.

What's for lunch... are there places to get food close by?

There are many convenient lunch locations around Midtown. A few of our faves include Clever Koi, Lux on Central, Pane Bianco, Flower Child, and Bosa Donuts. There are plenty more, so when you visit don’t forget to ask one of our teammates their favorite spot.

What's makes CO+HOOTS different than any other coworking space?

COnnection, COllaboration, and COmmunity with other amazing human beings. We are more than just a space and offer opportunities to collaborate on projects, share resources and generate more success to support every entrepreneur.

What types of events are available to attend?

From happy hours to educational programming, we offer a variety of free events to our members. We also keep you in the know about public events happening inside and out of our coworking space as well.

Where can I park my car / bike?

We have free parking for members below our building in any space that is not marked as reserved. Bike racks are available next to our main entrance, and at our Phoenix location, GRID Bikeshare has a hub conveniently located just outside our gates.

Are you dog friendly?

We are dog friendly, to friendly dogs. Please keep your dog on a leash, no barking, no biting, and no use of the furniture. Poor behavior will result in dog suspension.
