Our Learning Programs

Building a business is isolating and scary. It’s a problem CO+HOOTS has solved for hundreds of entrepreneurs and business owners, aspiring and established alike. From immersive workshops to full incubation services and weekly speaker series, we are here to meet you where you are in your entrepreneurial journey and surround you with resources that lead to your continued success.

It is up to us to collectively break down systematic barriers that adversely impact underrepresented business owners. When we build an ecosystem optimized for all, our entire city, state, nation collectively prospers.

Introducing

CO+HOOTS INSTITUTE™

for Entrepreneurship at

CO+HOOTS and Benedictine University Mesa have partnered to develop an intensive entrepreneur immersion program that will nurture, improve and accelerate students’ entrepreneurial traits and skills. The CO+HOOTS Institute for Entrepreneurship at Benedictine University Mesa is a partnership and program that is the only one of its kind in the U.S.

Available starting Fall 2020
